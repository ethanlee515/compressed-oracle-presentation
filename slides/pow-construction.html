Proof of Work: Construction
Small sidetrack for warm-up
Construction:
Let $n$ be some difficulty parameter
Verifier picks random $x\in\set{0, 1}^\lambda$ and sends it to prover.
Prover computes $y$ where $H(x, y)$ starts with $n$ zeroes.
Prover sends $y$ to verifier who checks $H(x, y)$.
Properties:
Hard to compute
Easy to check
