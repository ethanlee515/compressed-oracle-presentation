Proof of exercise
$[\![\neg P_0\xrightarrow{k,q}P_q]\!]=\sup_{U_i} ||P_q cO^k U_{q-1} cO^k\ldots U_1 cO^k (I-P_0)||$
$\qquad=\sup_{U_i} ||P_q cO^k (P_{q-1}+(I-P_{q-1})) U_{q-1} cO^k\ldots U_1 cO^k (I-P_0)||$
$\qquad=\sup_{U_i} ||P_q cO^k P_{q-1} U_{q-1} cO^k\ldots U_1 cO^k (I-P_0)||$
$\qquad\qquad+\sup_{U_i} ||P_q cO^k (I-P_{q-1}) U_{q-1} cO^k\ldots U_1 cO^k (I-P_0)||$
$\qquad\leq\sup_{U_i} ||P_{q-1} cO^k\ldots U_1 cO^k (I-P_0)||$
$\qquad\qquad+\sup_{U_i} ||P_q cO^k (I-P_{q-1})||$
$\qquad=[\![\neg P_0\xrightarrow{k,q-1}P_{q-1}]\!]+[\![\neg P_{q-1}\xrightarrow{k}P_q]\!]$
