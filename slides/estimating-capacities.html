Estimating transition capacities
We can bound transition capacities in terms of classical probabilities.
Let $L_i$ be local properties that strongly recognize $\neg P\rightarrow P'$, then we have $$[\![P\xrightarrow{k}P']\!]\leq\max_{x, D}\sqrt{10\sum_i P[U\in L_i]}$$
More general version where $L$ non-uniformly weakly recognize $\neg P\rightarrow P'$: $$[\![P\xrightarrow{k}P']\!]\leq\max_{x, D}e\sqrt{10\sum_i P[U\in L_i^{x, D}]}$$
