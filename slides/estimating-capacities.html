Estimating transition capacities
We can bound transition capacities in terms of classical probabilities.
Let $\mathsf{L}_i$ be local properties that strongly recognize $\neg\mathsf{P}\rightarrow\mathsf{P}'$, then we have $$[\![\mathsf{P}\xrightarrow{k}\mathsf{P}']\!]\leq\max_{x, D}\sqrt{10\sum_iP[U\in\mathsf{L}_i]}$$
More general version where the $\mathsf{L}$'s non-uniformly weakly recognize $\neg \mathsf{P}\rightarrow\mathsf{P}'$: $$[\![\mathsf{P}\xrightarrow{k}\mathsf{P}']\!]\leq\max_{x, D}e\sqrt{10\sum_i P[U\in L_i^{x, D}]}$$
