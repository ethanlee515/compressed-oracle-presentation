Proof of Sequential Work: Construction
As in [CP18].
See paper for graph construction. (p. 25)
For $x\in\set{0, 1}^\lambda$, define vertex label as $$\ell(v)=H(LbQ, x, v, \ell(in(v)))$$
Protocol:
Verifier samples $x\in\set{0, 1}^\lambda$ and sends it to the prover.
Prover compute all labels.
Verifier chooses challenge $c$ as a subset of leaves Prover generates challenge $c=H(ChQ, \ell(rt))$
Prover sends labels on the authentication path of $c$.
