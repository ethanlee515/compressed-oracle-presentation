Analyzing Algorithms by Transition Capacities
Previous result by Zhandry.
Rephrased in language of transition capacities.
Let $R\subseteq X^\ell\times Y^\ell$ be a relation.
Let $\mathcal{A}$ be some algorithm that, with success probability $p$, outputs $(\vec{x}, \vec{y})\in R$ where $H(x)=y$.
Then we have $$\sqrt{p}\leq[\![\![\bot\xrightarrow{k, q} \mathsf{P}^R]\!]\!]+\sqrt{\frac{\ell}{M}}$$ where $\mathsf{P}^R=\set{D | D\cap R\ne\emptyset}$.
