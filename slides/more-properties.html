Ways to Simplify Transition Capacities
For all properties $P, Q, P''$, $$ [\![P\xrightarrow{k}P'']\!]\leq [\![P\xrightarrow{k} P''\setminus Q]\!]+ [\![P\xrightarrow{k} P''\cap Q]\!] $$
Let $k=k'+k''$, then $$ [\![P\xrightarrow{k}P'']\!]\leq [\![P\xrightarrow{k'}P']\!]+ [\![\neg P'\xrightarrow{k''}P'']\!] $$
Let $X=X' \cup X''$, then $$ [\![P\xrightarrow{k} P''|X]\!]\leq [\![P\xrightarrow{k} P'|X']\!]+ [\![\neg P'\xrightarrow{k} P''|X'']\!] $$
