Reasoning about Databases
Finally done with the preliminaries!
A database property $\mathsf{P}\subseteq\mathcal{D}$ is formally modeled as a subset of databases.
Think of it as a predicate. $D\in\mathsf{P}$ iff $D$ satisfies $\mathsf{P}$.
By abuse of notation, also identified with the corresponding projector on the space of databases.
$\mathsf{P}(D)=D$ iff $D\in\mathsf{P}$
$\mathsf{P}(D)=0$ iff $D\notin\mathsf{P}$
